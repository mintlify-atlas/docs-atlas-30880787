Zero is an open-source email application built for people who want full control over their email experience. It unifies multiple email accounts into a single inbox, brings AI assistance directly into your workflow, and gives you the freedom to self-host your own instance—with no data tracking, no vendor lock-in, and no hidden agendas.

## What is Zero?

Zero replaces your email client while keeping your existing providers. You sign in with your Gmail or Outlook account, and Zero syncs your mail into a fast, unified interface that you control. You can run it on your own infrastructure or use the hosted version at [0.email](https://0.email).

Key capabilities:

- **Unified inbox** — read and reply to mail from multiple accounts in one place
- **AI composition** — draft emails, summarize threads, and search your mailbox with natural language
- **Privacy first** — Zero does not track, collect, or sell your email data
- **Open source** — MIT-licensed, fully transparent, community-driven
- **Self-hostable** — run on Docker or Cloudflare Workers with your own database

## How Zero works

Zero is a full-stack TypeScript monorepo. The frontend is a React Router v7 application built with Vite and deployed to Cloudflare Pages. The backend is a tRPC API server running on Cloudflare Workers. Email data is synced from providers like Gmail using their native APIs and stored in Cloudflare Durable Objects and R2—so reads are fast and your data stays under your control.
